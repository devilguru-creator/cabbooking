Feature 1: Cab Dispatching System

Cab Booking System features an efficient cab dispatching system. This system assigns cabs to the nearest available driver. Drivers are continuously monitored through GPS, ensuring the most accurate dispatch possible.

Feature 2: Customer Reviews and Ratings

Our system enables customers to provide ratings and reviews for their cab rides. These ratings help drivers improve their services, resulting in a better overall experience for customers.

Feature 3: Seamless Integration

Cab Booking System offers seamless integration with other online platforms, allowing users to easily access our services. The system is also compatible with popular smartphone applications.
